Yoga Day 2021

Authentic Yoga for All!

During and After the Pandemic

After our roaring success in 2020, we are back to celebrate the International Day of Yoga when we need it the most. This year's event will focus on how to incorporate yoga into our daily lives for physical, mental and spiritual growth in these challenging times. Highly experienced yoga teachers will lead asanas (physical postures), pranayama (breathwork), relaxation, and mantra meditation free of charge. See you on the mat!

Can’t make it? No worries, we will be recording and posting the live events.

Suggested preparations for the event:

  • Find a quiet place where you will be uninterrupted. Plan to stay for the entire event
  • If possible, please have a yoga mat, firm pillow or bolster for sitting, and a little bit of water
  • Wear loose, comfortable clothing made of natural fibers preferably
  • Yogic practices are best done on an empty to light stomach
  • For those who are menstruating, inversion poses should be avoided
  • While this event will be catered to everyone regardless of their experience or ability, it is always important to know individual limitations and respect them. Do not overexert at any point.
